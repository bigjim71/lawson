Last Stage

Following the shortlist we usually expect that the client will want to meet several candidates again and we will try to agree with the client the logistics for those meetings before (or very soon after) the shortlist occurs.

We also believe we can help in ensuring the job offer is accepted. Our role in the recruitment process allows both parties to voice expectations and concerns with us that might be challenging to address directly at this point.
